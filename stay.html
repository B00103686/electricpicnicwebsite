Public Camping...the traditional way to do EP! Enjoy the outdoors with friends in your very own tent! General Campsite and Family Campsite will open at 9am Friday, 29th August 2019 and will close at 1pm Monday, 2nd September 2019. Everybody is welcome! Be sure to get there quick.. spaces go fast.
Kip ‘n’ Go is the answer to one of the biggest issues festivals have to deal with – the left over tents! With a pre-pitched tent for 2 people ready and waiting for your arrival, and packed down after your departure, at only €7.5pp each night you are able to camp in comfort and help our environment.
Boutique Camping will give you a calm and relaxed sanctuary to unwind. The pre-erected tented village will have 24 hour security, luxury maintained toilets and showers, complimentary phone charging, pamper room as well as food and drink facilities.
